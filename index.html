💡 Your Guide

👨 Why 1,650 kcal for him?

  • Height 5'6" (168cm), Weight 83kg
  • BMR ≈ 1,850 kcal (Mifflin-St Jeor)
  • At moderate activity: ~2,300 kcal/day
  • 500–600 kcal deficit → ~0.5kg loss/week
  • At 1,650 kcal → lose ~5kg in 10–12 weeks

👩 Her: 1,300 kcal plan

  • Breakfast: 250–300 kcal
  • Lunch: 350–400 kcal
  • Dinner: 400–450 kcal
  • Snack: 100–150 kcal
  • Aim for 100g+ protein daily to preserve muscle

🥦 Eat More of These

  • Lean protein: chicken, fish, eggs, tofu, lentils
  • Non-starchy veg: broccoli, spinach, zucchini, cucumber
  • High-volume, low-cal: soups, salads, plain yogurt
  • Fiber: oats, legumes (keeps you full!)

🚫 Reduce These

  • Sugary drinks, juices, fancy coffees
  • Fried food & takeaway
  • White bread, pastries, sweets
  • Large portions of rice/pasta
  • Alcohol (empty calories!)

💧 Daily Essentials

  • Drink 2–2.5L water daily
  • Move at least 30 min (walk counts!)
  • Sleep 7–8 hours (affects hunger hormones)
  • Don't skip meals — it causes overeating later
  • Weigh yourselves weekly, same time, same day
